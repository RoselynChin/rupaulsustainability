Reusing Common Household Items

Item 1: Egg Cartons

You don't have to throw away your egg cartons - instead, you can repurpose them to store other items or use them as an arts and crafts project! You can decorate your carton with paint and glitter, and create something fun either by yourself or with others! Also, you can hold things such as marbles, makeup products, or even your Christmas ornaments! The round mounds are a perfect size for baubles during the most wonderful time of the year. RuPaul loves storing his Chanel ornaments in his Erewhon egg cartons.

Item 2: Utensils

Stop using plastic forks and spoons. Even the paper ones suck (they disintegrate after a few minutes, and RuPaul hates them). Instead, use metal or wooden utensils. RuPaul knows that you might be too lazy to wash them, especially if you're a college student dealing with constant exams and homoework. However, these reusable utensils will be more sustainable in the long term.

Item 3: Jars (and Bottles and Cups)

If you just finished using that tomato sauce, or have jars or containers laying around, then use them! There are several many uses for jars and similar items such as storage for personal items or food / drinks. RuPaul always says that mason jars are great for holding some flags.
